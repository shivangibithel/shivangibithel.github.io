Steganography

I love to learn new things in technology as a result I did a small course in ethical hacking, where I learned this beautiful technique of sending message called Steganography. Steganography is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image, or video. Let me show you a practical example of my work, here the image attached looks like a simple image. But if you download the image and open it using note pad++, you can see a hidden message at the end of the file.
